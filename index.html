Multi-Objective Particle Swarm Optimization based Design Space Exploration

Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) is a heuristic search methodology that tries to imitate the travels of a flock of birds aiming at finding food. PSO is based on a population of particles flying through a multi-dimensional search space. Each particle possesses a position and a velocity. And both variables are changed to emulate the social psychological tendency to impersonate the success of other individuals in the population. Here this PSO has been used to optimize Application Specific Processors.
